This website provides an overview of various 21st-century literary genres, highlighting their unique characteristics and notable works.

Genres

Hypertext Poetry

A digital form of poetry that uses hyperlinks to add layers of meaning.

Mobile Phone Text-tula

Poetry written and shared via mobile phone text messages.

Chick Lit

A genre that addresses modern women's lives, focusing on personal growth and relationships.

Speculative Fiction

Encompasses science fiction, fantasy, and other genres that explore imaginative concepts.

Flash Fiction

Short, concise stories typically under 1,000 words.

Blogs

Online journals or informational websites displaying information in reverse chronological order.

Graphic Novels

Stories presented in a comic-strip format and published as books.

Digi-fiction

Combines traditional book narrative with digital elements such as websites and videos.

Manga

Japanese comic books and graphic novels with a unique art style.

Doodle Fiction

Combines handwritten text and drawings to tell a story.
